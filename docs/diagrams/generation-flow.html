Actors — institutions & characters decide · populations respond · assets are acted through

PolityDONE

Territorial sovereign: territory = union of its ports' service domains (derived, never stored); taxes, wars, diplomacy, treaty ladder, vassalage, federation.

  • interior live (slice G): factions, drifting ideology, leadership — temperament = species × ideology × ruler × faction pressure
CorporationDONE

Emergent, non-territorial: niches → charters → portfolios, dividends, lobbying; nationalized with news fallout (slice I); outlaw cousins (cartels, pirate bands); Salvors strip battlefields.

CharacterDONE

Sparse: role-holders + event-born notables (founders, war heroes, explorers, magnates…). Personality colors decisions; mortality forces succession; dynasties marry and lapse into claims.

PopulationDONE

Species/culture segments, port-addressed; the demand side, the legitimacy base; migrates, starves, sickens (machine minds excepted), inherits.

FactionDONE

Semi-actor pressure bloc; graduates into polity (schism/civil war), corporation (charter), or the ruler role (coup) — the one factory for new institutions.

AssetsDONE

Infrastructure — the port (projects territory, carries lanes) + mines, shipyards, fortresses — and fleets (design lineages, six postures, freight, war, news carriage). Power concrete & conserved.

Cross-cutting state & interfaces

Event logDONE

Single global append-only stream, full grammar: actor ids, hex location, world-year, magnitude, visibility, 60+ typed payloads — deep time through history in one structure.

  • views computed over it: per-place, per-actor, biographies, era detection (slice I)
RegistriesDONE

Ports, lanes, facilities, designs, fleets, wreckage, segments, markets, loans, characters, dynasties, factions, corporations, relations, wars, beliefs, pulses, POIs, plagues — 22 artifact layers, byte-stable.

ControllerDONE

Decide(perceived) → policies + acts. AI and player interchangeable at every scope: character ⇄ corporation ⇄ polity (P2). Handover certification is slice J.

Price signalDONE

Market prices are the one valuation everything reads: expansion scoring, war goals (price shocks arm casus belli), migration, investment, niches.
