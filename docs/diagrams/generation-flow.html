Frame

The Four Clocks

each hands the next a finished board + latent story material
CosmicSPEC

~14 Gyr, ~150 steps. Potential prior + field stack (gas, star cohorts, metals, remnants) + features (mergers, globulars, nebulae, AGN). → the physical galaxy

→
EvolutionarySPEC

~Gyrs, ~Myr steps. Biosphere field → sapient origins → emergence schedule; precursor waves (grand + pocket) via coarse civ-arc sim → archaeology + living residue. → the living galaxy

→
GenerationalFRAME

~1,000y in ~25y epochs (a reign ≈ 1–3 epochs). History: polities, economies, wars, culture — the epoch sim below. → world-state handoff

→
PlayGAME

Days–weeks per tick. Same rules, finer sampling; news at ship speed; the player assumes any controller slot.

Actors — institutions & characters decide · populations respond · assets are acted through

PolityPROTO

Territorial sovereign: territory = union of its ports' service domains (derived, never stored); taxes, wars, diplomacy.

  • gains an interior: factions, drifting ideology, leadership — temperament becomes species × ideology × ruler (pass 4)
CorporationSPEC

Emergent, non-territorial: owns assets across borders, exerts influence; chartered / taxed / nationalized / expelled (pass 2).

CharacterSPEC

Sparse: role-holders + event-born notables. Personality colors institutional decisions; mortality forces succession; dynasties (pass 4).

PopulationPROTO

Species-tagged, hex-addressed; the demand side, the legitimacy base, the thing that migrates.

  • mixed species/culture per cell; migration (pass 4)
FactionSPEC

Semi-actor pressure bloc; graduates into polity (schism), corporation (charter), or the ruler role (coup) — the one factory for new institutions (pass 4).

AssetsSPEC

Infrastructure — the port (projects territory, carries lanes) + mines, shipyards, depots — and fleets (freight, war, news carriage; off-lane endurance a class axis). Power concrete & conserved (passes 1–3).

Cross-cutting state & interfaces

Event logDONE

Single global append-only stream, 9 types live.

  • formal event grammar: actor ids, location, world-year, magnitude, typed payload (pass 6)
RegistriesDONE

Polities (extinct-retained), wars, per-cell state — schema v5.

  • gain corporations, characters, fleets, infrastructure
ControllerSPEC

Decide(perceived) → policies + acts. AI and player interchangeable at every scope: character ⇄ corporation ⇄ polity (P2).

Price signalSPEC

Market prices replace hand-rolled "system value" as the one valuation everything reads: expansion, war goals, migration, investment.
