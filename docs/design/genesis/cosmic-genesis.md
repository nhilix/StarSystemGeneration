# Cosmic Genesis

*Status: awaiting design pass (0a). This stub states scope only.*

The cosmic clock: a deep-time structure-formation simulation over the coarse
lattice — nebulae, coalescence, stellar generations, supernova enrichment cycles.

Defines, when designed:

- The evolved fields that replace the analytic galactic-shape function and one-shot
  noise: element/metallicity distribution, density structure (pockets, filaments,
  voids), mineral-rich vs. gas-rich regions.
- The step model (span, step size, per-step processes) and its determinism keys.
- Output contract: **the physical galaxy** — the board consumed by the evolutionary
  clock and by Tier-1/Tier-3 generation (star-type leans, belt/anchor biases).
