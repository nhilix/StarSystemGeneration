# Life & Precursors

*Status: awaiting design pass (0b). This stub states scope only.*

The evolutionary clock: life across the galaxy at ~Myr steps, on the physical galaxy
produced by [cosmic-genesis.md](cosmic-genesis.md).

Defines, when designed:

- Biosphere seeding and spread, evolutionary progressions, extinctions and
  catastrophes, sapience maturation.
- **The emergence schedule**: which homeworlds reach spaceflight at which
  generational epoch — the source of asymmetric polity entry.
- **Precursor civilizations as previous emergence waves**: earlier sapients that
  rise, spread, and end at evolutionary timescale; their simulated residue (sites,
  machine descendants, uplifted species) is the archaeology layer.
- Output contract: **the living galaxy** — biosphere map, evolutionary states,
  emergence schedule, archaeology layer.
