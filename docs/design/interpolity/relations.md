# Relations & Diplomacy

*Status: awaiting design pass (5). This stub states scope only.*

How polities relate short of war.

Defines, when designed:

- Contact: how polities meet (expansion fronts, trade reach, news), and first-stance
  formation.
- The relations ladder: trade → alliance → federation, with mechanical teeth at each
  rung; the consent model (diplomacy matched in Resolution, both sides agreeing).
- Federations: merge gates (sustained alliance, openness, ideological compatibility,
  cohesion) and the fused polity's composition.
- Vassalage: tribute, defensive obligation, expansion limits, absorption and
  secession paths.
- Sanctions and tariffs as relations actions constraining the economy.
- How blockade strain, reputation, and faction pressure feed relations.
