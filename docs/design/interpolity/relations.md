# Contact, Relations & Diplomacy

How polities meet and relate short of war. Relations state is the pressure gauge
war reads and the ladder peace climbs.

## Contact

Polities meet when reach overlaps — expansion, trade range, or news of each other
(you can hold a stance on an empire you've never touched). First contact is an
event; the initial stance composes from each side's temperament composition,
species/culture strangeness filtered through openness, and any reputation that
arrived ahead of the meeting (P3).

## Relations state per pair

- **Warmth** — accumulated positive interdependence: trade volume, dynastic ties,
  honored alliances, shared wars won. Cooled by strain, seizures, atrocity news,
  ideology gap.
- **Tension** — accumulated friction: contested-influence overlap zones (the
  space model's organic borders), standing **claims** (succession claims, cultural
  kin under the other's rule, lost-territory grudges), interdiction strain,
  faction agitation, ideology gap × zeal. Tension decays only when its sources
  resolve — it is the war-pressure gauge.
- **Treaties** — discrete rungs, mutual consent in Resolution, each with teeth:

| Rung | Teeth |
|---|---|
| Trade pact | tariff cuts, lane priority |
| Non-aggression | spark de-escalation, tension damping |
| Defense alliance | join defensive wars — real deterrence (attackers price allied fleets) |
| Federation / Vassalage | below |

Warmth gates ascent; breaking any rung is a reputation event the galaxy hears.

## Federation

The merge gate: sustained alliance + high warmth + ideology compatibility +
openness + both cohesions healthy. The fused polity is **new** — multi-species
membership, population-weighted composition, fresh name, government form from the
combined ideology — and plays subsequent epochs as itself. Treaty-built
federations are structurally stabler than conquest empires: their segments chose
membership, so legitimacy starts high.

## Vassalage

The asymmetric rung: imposed by war settlement, or **chosen** by small polities
under threat (protection has a market). Terms: tribute (income share), defensive
obligation, foreign-policy lock. Two exits, both events: **absorption** (long
stable vassalage + cultural drift → peaceful annexation) and **secession**
(overlord weakness triggers an independence bid, negotiated or fought).

## Dynastic instruments

Marriages and wardships between dynastic-form polities buy warmth and create
**succession claims** — tension sources pointed the other way. The instrument
that secures peace this generation seeds a war of succession two reigns later.
Rare personal unions (one ruler, two thrones) are a federation fast-path with a
built-in crisis when the union breaks.

## P1 evidence

- **Legible residue**: relations map layer (warmth/tension shading), treaty
  registry on the polity panel, first-contact and treaty events in the chronicle.
- **Inhabitable state**: border tensions are visible before wars start; a
  political-agent player reads exactly which frictions are loaded and can work
  the rungs.
