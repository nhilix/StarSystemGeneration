# War

*Status: awaiting design pass (5). This stub states scope only.*

War fought with fleets over infrastructure.

Defines, when designed:

- **Causes**: economic (deficits, chokepoints, resource value), ideological
  (incompatible ideologies, faction pressure), and spark events — wars are not all
  economically rational.
- Goals and their legibility ("the Ore War of epoch 6").
- **Conduct**: fleet postures, fronts, battles, sieges, blockades; strength as fleet
  composition × tech × command; infrastructure as objective and casualty.
- Weariness, termination, and settlement: victory, white peace, vassalization,
  restoration; extinction rules.
- War's residue: scarring, ruins (battlefield POIs), veteran commanders, grudges
  feeding stances and factions.
