# Polity Interior

*Status: awaiting design pass (4). This stub states scope only. Expected to split
into focused documents (demographics, factions, characters, government) as the
design lands.*

Everything inside a polity's borders that isn't the economy: the people, the
politics, and the individuals.

Defines, when designed:

- **Demographics**: population growth, famine, standard of living; migration flows
  (pressure-driven movement, refugees, diasporas); mixed-species/mixed-culture
  cells and conquest composition.
- **Culture and ideology**: drifting polity ideology; cultural spread and
  divergence; government forms and how they change.
- **Factions**: interest blocs (ideological, regional, species-based,
  corporate-aligned), their strength model, pressure mechanics, and **graduation**
  (schism → polity, charter → corporation, coup → ruler change).
- **Characters**: generation and sparsity rules, personality model and how it colors
  institutional decisions, lifespan/succession/dynasties, event-born notables.
- Cohesion and stability: the composition-derived temperament
  (species × ideology × leadership) that feeds the polity's Intent AI.
