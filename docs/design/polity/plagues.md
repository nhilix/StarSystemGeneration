# Plagues & Contagion

Disease as a simulation vector — built almost entirely on existing machinery:
propagation on the lane graph, Medicine as mitigation, quarantine as self-imposed
interdiction, ideology and faction response, memorial residue.

## Origin

Outbreak odds per domain ∝ population density × SoL deficit × exposure — with two
flavored sources: frontier zoonoses (new biosphere contact) and **excavation
releases** (precursor sites and ruins; 0b's plague-ended waves make archaeology a
genuine biohazard). Seeded, rare; frequency a knob.

## Propagation

Contagion travels the lane graph with traffic — the news machinery carrying a
darker payload. Busy trade hubs are exposed first and worst; the wilds are
insulated by their isolation. Infection intensity is per-domain state that decays
with immunity buildup and suppression.

## Response

- **Medicine**: effective units (quantity × grade) suppress mortality and
  intensity; Life tech raises the ceiling. Plague years spike medicine demand and
  margins — a corporate opportunity and a smuggling market.
- **Quarantine**: a polity act — self-imposed lane closure (the sanction
  machinery pointed inward), trading interdiction strain on *yourself* for
  containment. Smugglers break quarantines: the black market thrives and spreads
  the plague — enforcement quality becomes a matter of life and death.

## Effects

Population shrink (mitigated by medicine, Life tech, SoL); SoL decline; ideology
shifts toward Sacral and Authority; prophet notables and sacral faction surges;
labor shortages that reprice wages and accelerate automation where Compute
allows.

## Resolution and residue

Plagues burn out through immunity or are suppressed by medicine. Residue:
demographic scars in the segment record, memorial POIs, chronicle events — and
era detection will name a bad one ("the Grey Years").

## P1 evidence

- **Legible residue**: infection map layer during; memorial POIs and demographic
  scars after; chronicle arcs with origin, path, and toll.
- **Inhabitable state**: quarantined ports, medicine-run contracts,
  blockade-running with a moral edge, excavation risk — plague is content at
  every scope.
