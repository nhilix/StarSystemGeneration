# The World-State Handoff

The final artifact layer: the live game's initial conditions, delivered whole and
in motion.

## Contents

1. **Complete registries**: polities (factions, segments, legitimacy),
   corporations, characters (roles, lineages), fleets (postures), facilities /
   ports / lanes (condition), markets (prices, black books), loans, treaties,
   live wars with objectives, POIs, and every actor's compressed perception
   state.
2. **Open threads, deliberately**: the final epoch is not tidied. Loaded
   tensions, pending successions, half-won wars, and leveraged corporations hand
   the player a world in motion, not a museum.

## The resumability contract (P7's final test)

The same state machine steps at play-tick resolution: the seven phases run on
fine steps, posted fleets resolve into voyages, prices drift between clearings,
characters become continuous individuals. **No genesis-only mechanic exists** —
certified by construction, since every mechanic in every pass is expressed in
world-year rates.

## Controller handover (P2's final test)

The player occupies any controller slot — a character's, a corporation's, a
polity's — by answering the same Intent-phase question the AI answers: *given
what you perceive, what are your policies and acts?* Scope changes are slot
changes; nothing inside the sim cares who is driving.

## The log never closes

The live game appends to the same event stream. The chronicle continues
seamlessly from deep time through history into the player's own deeds — which
propagate as news, accrue reputation, and can mint the player as a notable. The
galaxy's story and the player's story are one data structure.

## The delta boundary

Everything the live game mutates is recorded against the artifact as deltas plus
the continuing event log; the procedural baseline beneath (genesis strata, hex
tier) stays pure. A save is: GalaxyConfig + the artifact + the deltas + the log's
continuation.

## P1 evidence

The handoff is the inhabitability half of the two-customer test, delivered whole:
every quantity every pass designed — prices, stances, postures, claims, grades,
factions, biographies — is standing state a player at some scope can observe,
exploit, or change from tick one.
