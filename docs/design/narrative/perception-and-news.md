# Perception & News

*Status: awaiting design pass (6). This stub states scope only.*

The explicit model of what actors know (P3).

Defines, when designed:

- Per-actor perception state: stances, reputations, known prices, known wars;
  storage vs. compression (full beliefs vs. stances + knowledge horizons).
- News pulses: emission (Chronicle phase), traffic-borne propagation (speed emergent
  from shipping density), attenuation with distance, arrival and temperament-filtered
  reaction.
- Reputation: how witnessed actions (treaty broken, atrocity, liberation) accumulate
  into stance modifiers and gate diplomacy.
- Play-speed behavior: news at the speed of ships; the player as carrier.
