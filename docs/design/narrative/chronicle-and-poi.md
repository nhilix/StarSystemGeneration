# Chronicle, Eras & POIs

*Status: awaiting design pass (6). This stub states scope only.*

History as a readable product.

Defines, when designed:

- The **event grammar**: the one schema every subsystem emits (actor ids, location,
  world-year, magnitude, typed payload) and its vocabulary.
- Chronicle views at every zoom (P8): galaxy-scale era detection and naming, polity
  arcs, character lives.
- The **event→POI compiler**: which events compile into anchored hex-tier
  pre-commitments (battlefield ruins, dead cities, lost capitals, depleted mines,
  precursor strata), with epoch, magnitude, and participants attached.
- Indexing: per-cell and per-polity views over the single append-only log.
