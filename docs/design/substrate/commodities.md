# Commodities & Demand

*Status: awaiting design pass (1). This stub states scope only.*

The L0 goods layer: what exists to be produced, wanted, and traded.

Defines, when designed:

- The commodity vocabulary — the goods list (well beyond Provisions/Ore/Exotics),
  each with **use-cases** that create demand (population consumption by standard of
  living, industrial inputs, military production, tech investment).
- Production recipes: which cell conditions, populations, and infrastructure produce
  which goods, including embodiment relativity (aquatics farm ocean worlds; lithics
  barely need provisions).
- Population demand profiles per species/culture/standard-of-living.
- Market geography: where markets sit on the trade graph.
