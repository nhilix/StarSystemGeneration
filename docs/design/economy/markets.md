# Markets & Prices

*Status: awaiting design pass (2). This stub states scope only.*

The L1 price machinery: how supply meets demand and value emerges.

Defines, when designed:

- Price formation and clearing per market; how prices drift between clearings at
  play speed.
- Trade routing under prices and freight capacity (the fleet-capacity interface),
  constrained by tariffs, blockades, and sanctions.
- Wealth ledgers and taxation: how economic activity becomes polity income and
  corporate revenue; true wealth of a polity as an emergent quantity.
- Blockade strain as measured loss against the unconstrained baseline.
- The **price signal interface**: the valuations every other subsystem reads
  (expansion attractiveness, war-goal value, migration pull, investment siting).
